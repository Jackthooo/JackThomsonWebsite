Below are some of my works from classes, internships, and student ogranizations.

Brain Tumor Classifier

This was a final project for ECS 170: Introduction to Artificial Intelligence at UC Davis. Our team was able to build a convolutional neural network that could accurately identify the type of tumor present in a brain image, among glioma, meningioma, and pituitary tumors.

We were able to reach full accuracy using Keras to compile and train our model. I was responsible for researching preliminary datasets, writing preprocessing code, and debugging our data generation method to create augmented data for more training rounds.

SkyNote Landing Page

This was my first introduction to web design, which took place during an internship for a machine-learning based music education app. I had little previous experience with HTML and CSS, and had to learn from the existing code and online resources.

One of the biggest lessons I learned through this project was the importance of responsive web design, and ensuring that the site was user friendly regardless of screen size. I also would have liked to incorporate more design principles to enhance the site.

Neurotech Talk

Neurotech Talk is a neurotechnology focused podcast, discussing a wide range of topics from brain computer interfaces, artificial intelligence, and philosophy.

This project was spearheaded by the publications team that I lead at Neurotech@Davis. We had no previous experience with recording podcasts, but managed to work our way up to interviewing professors and professionals within the field of technology and neuroscience.
